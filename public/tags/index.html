A message from Rev Pauline

Welcome to St Alban’s and St Augustine’s Churches Toowoomba! Whether you are just here for a brief time or considering making St Alban’s or St Augustine’s your spiritual home, allow us to share with you, something about ourselves. Firstly: We value our tradition. Our services of worship are, for the most part, Eucharistic. That is, we focus our worship on the presence of Christ encountered in the bread and wine of the Service of Holy Communion. Secondly: We value you. We do so enjoy meeting new people, and if we can possibly find a reason to socialize with you (over a cup of tea or at one of our fundraisers,) we will! If you break bread with us you are one of the family. ​Thirdly: We value the grace of God at work in our lives. We live in a stunning place—the spectacular Toowoomba City—God has blessed us greatly. We would love to share it with you! Finally: We value the community in which we find ourselves. We want to offer you the gifts of worship, prayer and fellowship, all are welcome to be part of this wonderful and spirit filled community. Blessings Rev. Pauline
